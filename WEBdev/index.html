Real Impacts in Florida

From Miami to Jacksonville, communities are already feeling the effects of climate change

King Tides in Miami

Miami experiences "sunny day flooding" during king tides, where streets flood even without rain. This phenomenon is becoming more frequent and severe, affecting daily life and property values throughout South Florida.
